Frequently Asked Questions

22.05.2011

How can I become an administrator on my Company Page?

Administrative rights are automatically granted to the creator of a Company Page as part of the creation process.
19.05.2011

What can Company Page administrators do and how do I assign one?

Company administrators can edit Company Pages and assign other admins. If you're a Company Page administrator, you should see a blue Edit button in the top right corner of the page.
14.05.2011

How do I edit my Company Page?

You must be a Company Page administrator in order to edit your Company Page.
07.05.2011

How do employees show up on a Company Page?

Company Pages are designed to display current and former employees, alumni, or new hires. Only current employees should appear on your Company Page's landing page.
